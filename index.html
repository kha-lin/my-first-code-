My Goals for the Future

I created this simple website to share the goals I’m working toward and to keep myself motivated as I grow.

My Main Goals

  1. Improve my coding skills and build real projects.
  2. Strengthen my communication and problem-solving skills.
  3. Explore opportunities in tech and find a career I enjoy.
  4. Keep learning every day, even if it’s just one small thing.

How I Plan to Reach These Goals

  • Practice coding regularly by building small websites and apps.
  • Join online communities and challenges, like Goodwall, to stay active.
  • Watch tutorials, read articles, and take online courses to learn new concepts.
  • Ask for feedback from mentors, friends, and other learners.
  • Stay consistent and not give up when things feel difficult.
